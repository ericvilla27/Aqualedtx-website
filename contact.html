Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

A few questions we get asked often — answered upfront so you can make an informed decision.

How much does a typical irrigation installation cost? ▼
Irrigation system costs vary based on lot size, number of zones, water pressure, and controller type. For a typical residential install in the Buda/Kyle area, most systems run between $2,500 and $6,500. We'll walk through everything with you during the estimate before any commitment is made.
Do you offer free estimates? ▼
Yes — all estimates are free. We visit your property, assess what needs to be done, and put together a written quote with no pressure or obligation to proceed.
How long does an irrigation install take? ▼
Most residential irrigation systems are completed in 1 to 2 days. Larger properties or commercial sites may take longer. We'll give you a realistic timeline as part of your estimate.
Do you work on existing irrigation systems, or only new installs? ▼
Both. We repair and upgrade existing irrigation systems regularly — broken heads, zone failures, controller replacements, and line repairs. Most repair visits can be scheduled within a week.
Do you do commercial work? ▼
Yes. We've completed irrigation, lighting, drainage, and trenching projects for commercial properties throughout Central Texas. Contact us to discuss your project scope and timeline.
Are you licensed and insured? ▼
Yes. AquaLED, LLC is fully licensed in the state of Texas and carries general liability insurance. We're happy to provide proof of insurance before any job begins.
