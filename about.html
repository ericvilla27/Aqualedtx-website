Who We Are

Built in Central Texas. Built to Last.

AquaLED, LLC started because we saw a gap in the market — too many outdoor contractors in the Austin area were taking on more work than they could handle, giving homeowners the runaround, or cutting corners to finish faster. We decided to do it differently.

We're a small, focused team based in Buda, TX. We specialize in irrigation systems, LED landscape lighting, drainage solutions, and underground utility work — and we do each of those things well. We don't spread ourselves thin trying to be everything to everyone.

What that means for you: when you hire AquaLED, you're working directly with the people designing and installing your system. You can ask questions, change your mind before we start, and expect straight answers about cost, timeline, and what we recommend.

We've worked on everything from small backyard irrigation installs for homeowners in Kyle to multi-acre commercial irrigation systems in San Marcos. The job size doesn't matter — the work quality does.
