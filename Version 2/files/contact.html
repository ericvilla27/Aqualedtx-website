Common Questions

Good to Know Before You Reach Out

Do you offer free estimates? ▼
Yes — all estimates are free. We visit your property, assess what needs to be done, and provide a written quote. No obligation to proceed.
How fast can you start? ▼
It depends on current workload, but we work to get estimates scheduled quickly and most jobs begin within a reasonable timeframe after approval. We'll give you a realistic start date as part of your estimate.
How long do most jobs take? ▼
Most projects are completed in under a week. Residential irrigation installs are often done in 1–2 days. Larger commercial or multi-service jobs take longer — we'll outline the timeline clearly in your estimate.
Do you work on commercial properties? ▼
Yes. We have extensive commercial experience — including school campuses (Bowie High School, Martin Middle School), sports complexes (Dripping Springs), and multi-acre commercial sites. Contact us to discuss your project scope.
Are you licensed and insured? ▼
Yes. We hold a Texas Licensed Irrigator certification (LI #22379) and carry $2 million in general liability insurance. Proof of insurance is available on request before any job begins.
Do you do irrigation repairs, or only new installs? ▼
Both. We diagnose and repair existing systems — broken heads, zone failures, controller issues, and damaged lines. Many repairs can be completed in a single visit.
