Who We Are

Small Business. Big Capabilities.

AquaLED, LLC was built around a simple idea: Central Texas deserves an outdoor contractor that actually communicates, shows up prepared, and gets the job done without dragging it out or padding the quote.

We've spent five years building that reputation — one project at a time. From residential irrigation installs in Buda and Kyle to large-scale commercial excavation at Bowie High School and athletic field irrigation at schools and sports complexes across the region, our crew brings the same level of organization and craftsmanship to every job.

When you hire AquaLED, you work directly with the person in charge. There's no middleman, no franchise overhead, and no incentive to upsell you on things you don't need. We listen to what you actually need and quote exactly that.

Most of our projects are completed in under a week. That's not a marketing claim — it's how we run our operation. An organized crew and clear scope means we start on time, work efficiently, and finish before most contractors are done scheduling.

Licensed, Insured, and Operating by the Book

AquaLED, LLC holds a Texas Licensed Irrigator certification (LI #22379) and carries $2 million in general liability insurance on every project. We call 811 before every dig, pull permits when required, and don't cut corners that could create problems for you down the road. Your property is protected — and so is our reputation.
